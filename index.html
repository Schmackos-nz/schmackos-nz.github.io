⚜ WELCOME TO THE CHEST RUN ⚜

🗝️ Objective: loot all 16 chests. Opening your first chest starts a 5-minute timer — clear them all before it runs out. A brand-new map arrives at the top of every hour, the same for everyone.

🟢 Guardians patrol near some chests. They can't hurt you, but one smack interrupts your 1.5s opening cast. Jump up mounds with Space, climb ladders with W.

Once per run you may use each of these abilities:

🪤 [1] Snare Trap — drop it at your feet; the first guardian to step on it is stuck in place for the rest of the run.

⚡ [2] Plunder Surge — your next chest opens instantly, no cast to interrupt.

👻 [3] Phase Cloak — guardians can't see you for 5 seconds, or until you start opening a chest.

⚠ Spend them wisely — they don't come back until the next hour, and a wasted ability can mean a guarded chest you can't crack before the clock runs out.
