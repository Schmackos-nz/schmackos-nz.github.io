Classic Split Pea Soup

Serves 6 Time 1h 30m Difficulty Medium Soup

Ingredients

  • 500g dried green split peas
  • 1 smoked ham hock
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1.5L chicken stock
  • Salt, pepper, thyme

Method

  1. Sauté onion, carrot, celery in oil until soft.
  2. Add garlic, peas, stock, ham hock, and thyme.
  3. Simmer 1 hour until peas dissolve into a thick soup.
  4. Remove hock, shred meat, stir back in. Season to taste.

Chilled Pea & Mint Soup

Serves 4 Time 20m Difficulty Easy Soup

Ingredients

  • 600g frozen peas
  • 700ml vegetable stock
  • 1 bunch fresh mint
  • 200ml crème fraîche
  • 1 shallot, sliced
  • Lemon juice

Method

  1. Sweat shallot in butter, add stock and bring to boil.
  2. Add peas, cook 3 minutes, add mint.
  3. Blend until smooth, swirl in crème fraîche.
  4. Chill, season with lemon juice, salt and pepper.

Pea & Ricotta Bruschetta

Serves 4 Time 15m Difficulty Easy Starter

Ingredients

  • 200g fresh peas (or frozen, thawed)
  • 200g ricotta
  • 4 thick slices sourdough
  • Lemon zest + juice
  • Fresh mint, chilli flakes, olive oil

Method

  1. Crush peas roughly with a fork. Mix with ricotta, lemon zest, and mint.
  2. Toast bread, rub with garlic.
  3. Pile pea mix on top, drizzle olive oil, finish with chilli flakes.

Pea & Prawn Risotto

Serves 4 Time 40m Difficulty Medium Main

Ingredients

  • 350g arborio rice
  • 400g raw king prawns
  • 250g frozen peas
  • 1.2L hot fish stock
  • 1 onion, finely diced
  • 150ml dry white wine
  • 50g parmesan, grated
  • 50g butter

Method

  1. Fry onion in butter until translucent. Add rice, toast 2 minutes.
  2. Add wine, stir until absorbed. Add stock ladle by ladle, stirring constantly.
  3. Stir in peas and prawns in the final 5 minutes.
  4. Remove from heat, beat in butter and parmesan. Rest 2 minutes before serving.

Lamb Keema with Peas (Keema Matar)

Serves 4 Time 45m Difficulty Medium Main

Ingredients

  • 500g minced lamb
  • 250g frozen peas
  • 2 onions, finely diced
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, grated
  • 400g tin chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp each: cumin, coriander, turmeric
  • Fresh coriander, to serve

Method

  1. Brown onion in oil, add garlic and ginger, cook 2 minutes.
  2. Add spices, stir 1 minute. Add lamb, brown thoroughly.
  3. Add tomatoes, simmer 20 minutes. Add peas, cook 5 minutes more.
  4. Serve with basmati rice or naan.

Pasta e Piselli

Serves 4 Time 25m Difficulty Easy Main

Ingredients

  • 400g small pasta (ditalini or orzo)
  • 400g frozen peas
  • 150g pancetta or bacon, diced
  • 1 onion, finely diced
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 500ml chicken stock
  • Parmesan, pepper, olive oil

Method

  1. Fry pancetta until crisp. Add onion and garlic, soften.
  2. Add peas and stock, simmer 5 minutes.
  3. Blend half the mixture, return to pan.
  4. Cook pasta directly in the sauce, adding water as needed.
  5. Serve thick and soupy with parmesan and black pepper.

Pea & Spinach Shakshuka

Serves 4 Time 30m Difficulty Easy Main

Ingredients

  • 200g frozen peas
  • 100g baby spinach
  • 800g tin crushed tomatoes
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 red pepper, diced
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 tsp cumin, 1 tsp smoked paprika, ½ tsp cayenne
  • 6 eggs
  • Feta, to crumble on top

Method

  1. Sauté onion and pepper, add garlic and spices.
  2. Add tomatoes, simmer 10 minutes. Stir in peas and spinach.
  3. Make wells, crack in eggs. Cover and cook until whites are set.
  4. Crumble feta over, serve with crusty bread.

Fish Pie with Pea Mash

Serves 6 Time 1h Difficulty Medium Main

Ingredients

  • 800g mixed fish (salmon, cod, smoked haddock)
  • 300g frozen peas (for sauce) + 400g (for topping)
  • 1.2kg potatoes
  • 500ml whole milk
  • 50g butter + 50g flour (roux)
  • 1 leek, sliced
  • Parsley, lemon zest

Method

  1. Poach fish in milk, set aside. Make white sauce with butter, flour, and poaching milk.
  2. Fold in fish, peas (300g), leek, parsley, and lemon zest. Pour into baking dish.
  3. Boil potatoes, mash with butter. Blend in remaining peas for a green mash.
  4. Top pie with pea mash, bake 200°C for 25 minutes until golden.

Smashed Peas with Feta & Lemon

Serves 4 Time 10m Difficulty Easy Side

Ingredients

  • 400g frozen peas
  • 100g feta, crumbled
  • Lemon zest + juice
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Fresh mint, salt, pepper

Method

  1. Boil peas 2 minutes, drain.
  2. Roughly smash with a fork — keep it chunky.
  3. Fold in feta, lemon, mint, and a generous drizzle of oil.

Pea, Bacon & Potato Salad

Serves 6 Time 25m Difficulty Easy Salad

Ingredients

  • 800g baby potatoes
  • 200g frozen peas
  • 150g bacon, diced
  • 4 spring onions, sliced
  • 3 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 2 tbsp sour cream
  • Wholegrain mustard, cider vinegar

Method

  1. Boil potatoes until tender, halve. Blanch peas 2 minutes.
  2. Fry bacon until crisp.
  3. Mix mayo, sour cream, mustard, and vinegar for dressing.
  4. Toss potatoes, peas, bacon, and spring onions in dressing.

Pea & Mint Fritters

Serves 4 Time 20m Difficulty Easy Side

Ingredients

  • 300g frozen peas
  • 2 eggs
  • 80g plain flour
  • 50g parmesan, grated
  • Handful fresh mint, chopped
  • Oil for frying

Method

  1. Blanch and roughly crush peas.
  2. Mix with eggs, flour, parmesan, and mint into a thick batter.
  3. Fry heaped tablespoons in oil, 2–3 minutes per side until golden.
  4. Serve with yoghurt or sour cream.

Pea Hummus

Serves 6 Time 10m Difficulty Easy Snack

Ingredients

  • 400g frozen peas
  • 3 tbsp tahini
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt, cumin

Method

  1. Boil peas 3 minutes, drain and cool.
  2. Blend everything until smooth.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and a pinch of smoked paprika.
  4. Serve with pita, crackers, or vegetables.

Crispy Roasted Chickpea & Pea Bowl

Serves 2 Time 35m Difficulty Easy Snack

Ingredients

  • 200g frozen peas
  • 400g tin chickpeas, drained
  • Mixed greens, cucumber, radish
  • Tahini dressing (tahini, lemon, garlic, water)
  • Smoked paprika, olive oil

Method

  1. Toss chickpeas in olive oil and paprika, roast 200°C for 25 minutes.
  2. Blanch peas, cool.
  3. Assemble bowl with greens, peas, roasted chickpeas, and vegetables.
  4. Drizzle with tahini dressing.

Pea & Vanilla Ice Cream

Serves 6 Time 20m + freeze Difficulty Medium Dessert

Ingredients

  • 400g frozen peas
  • 400ml double cream
  • 200ml whole milk
  • 150g caster sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Method

  1. Blend peas with milk until completely smooth. Strain through a fine sieve.
  2. Whip cream to soft peaks, fold in pea mixture, sugar, vanilla, and salt.
  3. Churn in ice cream machine, or freeze in container, stirring every hour for 4 hours.
  4. Subtly green, naturally sweet — serve with shortbread.
