TALLOWMINE

You are lost in the black of an abandoned mine, with nothing but a guttering tallow candle. The dark is not empty.

Find the SPADE and the DYNAMITE at opposite ends of the shaft. Dig through the collapsed rock, blast the wall behind it, and crawl out into the night.

When your candle dies you must relight it — but the light always wakes something. Run. Hide in the cupboards. Do not let it see you go in.
