TALLOWMINE

You are lost in the black of an abandoned mine, with nothing but a guttering tallow candle. The dark is not empty.

Climb the ladders, find the SPADE and the DYNAMITE at opposite ends of the shaft. Dig through the collapsed rock, blast the wall behind it, and crawl out into the night.

If your candle dies, something comes for you in the black — relight it fast. And when you catch it standing in the dark ahead of you, do not get close. It is already learning where you are. Run. Hide. Don't let it see you go in.
