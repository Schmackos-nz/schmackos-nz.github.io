A Villager's Life

You are a humble villager. Around you, a township will rise in real time — builders raising houses, mills and barracks, soldiers patrolling the walls of a future castle.

Nobody is going to feed you. Gather wood, berries and stone, hunt deer and boar, mine gold ore in the far hills — and when the town's buildings open, apply for a job: lumberjack, farmer, miner, builder, guard, knight, or entertainer. Meet your daily quota and get paid gold at dusk. Gold buys food at the market, so you can retire from foraging.

Beware wolves at night — and watch the minimap: foreign banners camp far beyond the hills. Their scouts will find your town eventually, and their envoys do not take refusal kindly.
